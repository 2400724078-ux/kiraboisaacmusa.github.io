Welcome to Our Marine Life Project

Uganda, known as the Pearl of Africa, is home to rich aquatic biodiversity within its lakes and rivers. From the great Nile perch to vibrant cichlids, these ecosystems support not only marine species but also human livelihoods.

Environmental Challenges

Pollution, overfishing, and invasive species threaten the balance of Uganda’s marine ecosystems. Conservation and awareness are crucial to protect these precious resources.
